Forging Tomorrow's Elite.

FEN Investment Group (FIG) is the student-run investment community of the Faculty of Economics and Business at Universidad de Chile. We train the next generation of investors through hands-on portfolio management, industry visits, open education and a flagship portfolio tournament run on Bloomberg terminals.

Flagship event

Portfolio Tournament 2026.

Sixty-three student teams manage simulated USD 10 million portfolios invested in iShares/BlackRock ETFs, benchmarked against the MSCI ACWI and scored on Bloomberg PORT metrics — Information Ratio, excess return, Sharpe, VaR and maximum drawdown. Rankings are published every Friday; the top five teams defend their management before an expert jury at the final on November 9, 2026.
